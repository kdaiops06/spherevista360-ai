## Welcome to SphereVista360

SphereVista360 is an AI-powered financial intelligence platform designed to deliver automated, data-driven insights across markets, currencies, and macroeconomics.

## What We Offer

### 📰 AI-Generated News & Analysis

Our AI agents continuously monitor financial markets, economic data, and news sources to generate comprehensive analysis articles. Every article is:

- **Data-driven** — sourced from Alpha Vantage, FRED, and premium news APIs
- **SEO-optimized** — structured for discoverability
- **Automated** — published daily via GitHub Actions

### 📊 Financial Tools

Free, fast, and accurate calculators:

- **Currency Converter** — real-time exchange rates for 150+ currencies
- **Inflation Calculator** — see how inflation erodes purchasing power
- **Compound Interest Calculator** — project investment growth
- **Purchasing Power Calculator** — compare dollar value across decades

### 🧠 AI Market Predictions

Our prediction engine analyzes economic indicators, market trends, and sentiment data to generate outlooks for major assets including stocks, currencies, commodities, and crypto.

### 💱 Currency Analytics

Real-time exchange rates and currency strength analysis covering major and emerging market currencies.

## How It Works

SphereVista360 runs on a fully automated pipeline:

1. **Data Collection** — Financial data APIs feed real-time information
2. **AI Analysis** — Claude and GPT-4 agents analyze and generate content
3. **Publishing** — GitHub Actions commit articles and trigger deployment
4. **Delivery** — Next.js serves optimized content via Vercel's edge network
